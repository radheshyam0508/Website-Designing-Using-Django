ABOUT US

Happy To Introduce

Splendid Bioanalytics (SBs) provides customize and user specific data science analytical algorithms, Apps and software on Machine learning and deep learning approaches by using different programming languages like python, Java, R, MATLAB and bash. Sbs addresses data science difficult challenges by expertise who has decades of experience in different fields like IoT, Bioinformatics, NGS Data Analysis, We provides basic research and up gradation in Application development in Anriod or IoS, Biotechnology, Pharmaceuticals, Environment Engineering, Chemical Engineering and Electronic and Communication. Our team's has experts who worked or got trained from prime research institutes of world like University of Cambridge (UK), University of Copenhegen (Denmark), Deutsches Krebsforschungszentrum, DKFZ, Germany, Denmark Technical University (Denmark), Banaras Hindu University (BHU, Varanasi, India), Centre for Cellular and Molecular Biology (Hyderabad, India) etc.

Motto of our firm is to linearized the research complexity, according to the objective of a scientific groups and provides detailed graphical and interactive output. We provide solutions of problems like, IoT, Machine learning, Deep learning apprroaches approaches Genome Assembly of all type of NGS data (Genomics and Transcriptomics), Gene Finding and Annotation, Marker Data Analysis (SSR, SNP etc.), Comparative Genomics, Prediction of Environmental Data (Air and Water Pollutions).
